Text of the current Dörtstani constitution

First article.

Dörtstan is an indivisible, secular, democratic and social Republic. It ensures equality in front of the law for all citizens, regardless of origin, race or religion. It respects all beliefs.

Article 2.

Dörtstan uses the 4 main languages ​​in use within its borders, French, Breton, English and Esperanto. To guarantee the fairest equality for all our citizens, all official texts must therefore be translated into each of these languages.
Its emblem is the number 4 behind which each citizen is united.

Article 3.

National sovereignty belongs to the people who exercise it through their representatives elected for life and through referendum.
No section of the people nor any individual can take responsibility for its exercise.
